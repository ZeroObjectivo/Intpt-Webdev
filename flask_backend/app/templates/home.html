About Us

Herons' Hub is a University of Makati digital community where students can share campus updates, discover opportunities, join events, and connect through verified school identities. Built for collaboration, it keeps discussions relevant, student-focused, and easy to navigate.

Features

Only UMak Students allowed

Access is limited to verified @umak.edu.ph accounts to keep the community safe and school-focused.

Community Posts

Share updates, marketplace offers, and questions in one moderated student feed.

Unified Platform

Events, scholarship listings, and UMak Coop catalogs are accessible in one place.

Student-Based Application

Built around student needs with campus-first features, reporting, and real-time interactions.
